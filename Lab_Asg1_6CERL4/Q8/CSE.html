Computer Engineering Department

As a pioneering institution in technical education, we take pride in nurturing the next generation of computer engineers who are poised to innovate, lead, and shape the future.

At IET-DAVV, our Computer Engineering Department is committed to excellence in both teaching and research. We offer a comprehensive curriculum that blends theoretical knowledge with hands-on practical experience, ensuring that our students are well-equipped to tackle real-world challenges in the ever-evolving field of computer engineering.

Our faculty members are accomplished scholars and industry experts who are passionate about sharing their knowledge and expertise with students. Through their guidance and mentorship, students have the opportunity to explore cutting-edge technologies and develop skills that are highly sought after in today's competitive job market.

In addition to our strong academic programs, the Computer Engineering Department at IET-DAVV is actively involved in research across a wide range of areas, including artificial intelligence, machine learning, cybersecurity, embedded systems, and more. Our faculty and students collaborate on projects that push the boundaries of innovation and contribute to the advancement of the field.
