Mechanical Engineering Department

Our department is dedicated to fostering excellence in education and research in the field of mechanical engineering, preparing students to become innovative problem solvers and leaders in the industry.

At IET-DAVV, our Mechanical Engineering Department offers a comprehensive curriculum that combines fundamental principles with advanced technologies, providing students with a strong foundation in mechanical engineering. From thermodynamics and fluid mechanics to machine design, manufacturing processes, and robotics, our programs cover a diverse range of topics to meet the evolving needs of the industry.

Our distinguished faculty members are renowned scholars, researchers, and industry experts who are committed to delivering a rigorous and engaging educational experience. Through their guidance and mentorship, students have the opportunity to explore their interests, pursue their passions, and develop the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in their careers.

In addition to academic programs, the Mechanical Engineering Department at IET-DAVV is actively involved in research and development activities, addressing key issues such as renewable energy, sustainable manufacturing, and advanced materials. Through research projects, industry collaborations, and internships, students have the opportunity to work on innovative solutions and contribute to advancements in the field.
