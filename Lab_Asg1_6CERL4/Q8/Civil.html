Civil Engineering Department

The Civil Engineering department is dedicated to providing a comprehensive education in civil engineering, preparing students to address the complex challenges of infrastructure development, construction, and environmental sustainability.

At IET-DAVV, we offer a diverse range of academic programs that blend theoretical knowledge with practical experience, equipping students with the skills and expertise needed to excel in the field of civil engineering. From structural analysis and design to transportation engineering, environmental engineering, and geotechnical engineering, our curriculum covers a broad spectrum of topics to meet the needs of the industry.

Our esteemed faculty members are renowned scholars, researchers, and industry experts who are committed to delivering high-quality education and mentorship to our students. Through their guidance and support, students have the opportunity to engage in hands-on projects, research initiatives, and industry collaborations, gaining invaluable insights and experiences that prepare them for successful careers in civil engineering.

In addition to academic excellence, the Civil Engineering Department at IET-DAVV is actively engaged in research and innovation, addressing pressing issues such as sustainable infrastructure development, disaster resilience, and urban planning. Our research endeavors aim to contribute to the advancement of civil engineering knowledge and practices, fostering positive impacts on society and the environment.
