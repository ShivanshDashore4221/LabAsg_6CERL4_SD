About Us

The Institute of Engineering & Technology (IET), is an engineering college of Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya, Indore. It was founded as an autonomous institute in 1996. It is an approved Institution by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi, Government of Madhya Pradesh and University Grants Commission (UGC). IET was started with a total intake of 90 students in three branches of Bachelor of Engineering (BE) but now it offers an undergraduate program in six disciplines with a total intake of 540 and Postgraduate programs in engineering and science too.

The institute recently concluded its 2022 placement drive with more than 90% placements. The 2021-22 batch got a better experience during its campus placement drive compared to the previous batch. This year, 427 students were placed which is more than double the number it was last year. The highest salary package stood at INR 57 LPA. Average salary increased from INR 5.23 LPA in 2021 to INR 6 LPA in 2022. the placement drive engaged 108 recruiters.
