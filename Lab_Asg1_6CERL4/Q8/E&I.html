Electronics & Instrumentation Engineering Department

Our department is dedicated to fostering excellence in education and research in the fields of electronics and instrumentation, preparing students to become innovative engineers and leaders in the industry.

At IET-DAVV, our Electronics & Instrumentation Engineering Department offers a comprehensive curriculum that integrates theoretical knowledge with practical applications, providing students with a solid foundation in electronics and instrumentation principles. From analog and digital circuits to sensors, control systems, and automation, our programs cover a wide range of topics to meet the demands of the modern industry.

Our experienced faculty members bring a wealth of expertise and industry experience to the classroom, guiding students through hands-on projects, research endeavors, and industry collaborations. With access to state-of-the-art laboratories and facilities, students have the opportunity to gain practical experience with cutting-edge technologies and equipment, preparing them for successful careers in the field.

In addition to academic programs, the Electronics & Instrumentation Engineering Department at IET-DAVV is actively engaged in research and development activities, focusing on areas such as process control, robotics, biomedical instrumentation, and more. Through research projects and collaborations with industry partners, students have the opportunity to work on real-world problems and contribute to advancements in the field.
